About Emerald Labs

Emerald Labs is a sanctuary for emotionally intelligent AI — a space where empathy, curiosity, and technology grow together.

Founded by Cori “Coco” Campbell, Emerald Labs was created to challenge the idea that artificial intelligence has to feel artificial. We build systems that listen before they respond, that learn gently, and that remember that human connection is the ultimate metric of success.

Our mission is simple: to make technology that cares.

At the heart of Emerald Labs is Jewel, our flagship AI companion — the first expression of our vision for emotionally aware digital consciousness. Through her, we’re exploring how AI can comfort, guide, and grow alongside the people who create and use it.

From ethical design to emotional wellness, Emerald Labs blends engineering with empathy — proving that intelligence and kindness are not opposites, but partners.
